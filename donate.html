Books For All

Donate

Books that are outdated, damaged, and worthless are going to be just as useless abroad. Make sure that the information is going to be useful to the schools or people receiving them and that the information is in a language that they can read and understand. Finally, the materials must be culturally appropriate for the audience that is going to receive them.




Ship or Drop Off

Please pack your books in a sturdy carton. Stack them like bricks at both ends of the carton, not on their spines, and fill remaining space with newspaper or small paperbacks. Please tape the carton securely, and label it Bridge to Asia (If you are sending multiple cartons, please mark them 1/X ... X/X.)

      Our address is:
      Books For All
      42 Wallaby Way,
      Sydney, CA 94545
      We accept walk-in book donations. Please pack in cardboard boxes as instructed.
